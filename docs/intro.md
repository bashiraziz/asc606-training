# How to use this site

Welcome to the **ASC 606 Revenue Recognition Hub** — a free, self-serve training and reference platform for accountants, controllers, auditors, and finance managers working with GAAP revenue recognition under ASC 606.

## What this site covers

This site provides complete, practical coverage of ASC 606 — *Revenue from Contracts with Customers* — including:

- A **10-module training manual** that walks through every element of the five-step model
- An **interactive worksheet** for analyzing individual contracts at inception
- A **month-end close tracker** for managing the full accrual-basis close cycle
- **Compliance examples** — both compliant scenarios and common non-compliant patterns with corrections

All content is based on the FASB codification (ASC 606-10) and related guidance, effective for periods beginning after December 15, 2017.

## Recommended learning path

Follow this sequence for the most effective experience:

1. **Training manual** — Start with Module 1 (Introduction) and work through all 10 modules in order. Each module builds on the previous one.
2. **Interactive worksheet** — After completing the manual, open the ASC 606 Worksheet and work through a real or hypothetical contract using the five-step model.
3. **Month-end close tracker** — Use the close tracker during or after an actual close cycle to see how the five steps translate into journal entries, reconciliations, and checklists.
4. **Compliance examples** — Review the compliant and non-compliant scenarios to test your judgment on edge cases.

## Who this is for

- **Staff and senior accountants** learning ASC 606 for the first time
- **Controllers and accounting managers** building or reviewing revenue recognition processes
- **Auditors** wanting a structured reference for client revenue reviews
- **Finance managers** who need a working understanding of how revenue is recognized under GAAP

## Privacy and data

All tools run entirely in your browser. No data is saved, transmitted, or stored anywhere. There is no account, no login, and no backend. When you close the tab, your work is gone — print or screenshot to retain it.

This is intentional. The tools are for analysis and learning, not record-keeping. Your accounting system is the system of record.

## A note on professional judgment

ASC 606 is a principles-based standard. Many of the judgments required — collectibility assessment, variable consideration constraint, distinct-good analysis — cannot be reduced to a checkbox. This site provides a structured framework, but the substance of your revenue recognition decisions requires professional judgment and review by a qualified accountant or auditor.

This site is not a substitute for professional accounting advice.
